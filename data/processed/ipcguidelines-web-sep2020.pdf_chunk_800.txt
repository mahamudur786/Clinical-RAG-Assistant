Before entering the operation room, don the cap, face mask and eyewear. Some masks 
come with eye protection; however, these may not be available in resource limited settings. A simple method of making a mask with eye protection is by stapling or ﬁ  xing a plastic transparent sheet to the mask so that when the mask is worn, the transparent sheet forms a shield in front of the eyes.

1. The scrubbing facility: tap and sink2. Remove rings, watch and bracelets.3. Clean the ﬁ  ngernails.
4. Starting with the ﬁ  ngers, apply soap/ antiseptic to all surfaces of hands. 
5. Rub between ﬁ  ngers.
6. Continue to apply soap/ antiseptic till the elbow. 7. Starting with ﬁ  ngers, rinse each hand and arm till the elbow with the hands above the level
