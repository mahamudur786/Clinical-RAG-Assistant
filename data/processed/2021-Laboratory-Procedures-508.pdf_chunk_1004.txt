1.3.4 Air Purge  
Additional purging of the workspace without user activity should be allowed for 2 to 3 minutes after 
materials and apparatus have been placed in it. This will rid the area of all “ loose ” contamination 
that may have been introduced with the items.  
1.3.5 Perform Work
