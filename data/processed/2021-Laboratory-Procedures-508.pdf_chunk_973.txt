10.4.3 Use of the Temperature Recording Instrument  
The Temp erature Guard system is a multiple device monitor and alarm system that is equipped with 
an integrated aut odialer and data logging capabilities. The Temperature Guard system automatically 
monitors the interior temperature of the refrigerators, freezers, and room temperature  in the 
laboratory and phlebotomy rooms. 
The system can monitor up to eight temperature sensors and each sensor must have a probe 
submerged into a bottle of resin.
