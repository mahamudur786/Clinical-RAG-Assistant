and to other patients, and 
x Reducing the risk of infection associated with receiving emergency care.
The basics of standard precautions including hand hygiene PPE, etc. should be 
strictly adhered to.
E. IPC in dialysis units
Infection is the most common cause of hospitalization and the second most common cause of mortality among haemodialysis (HD) patients, after cardiovascular disease. HD patients are exposed to different types of infection, which include BSI and localized infections of vascular access, blood-borne infections (HBV, HCV and/ or HIV) and airborne infections such as tuberculosis.
