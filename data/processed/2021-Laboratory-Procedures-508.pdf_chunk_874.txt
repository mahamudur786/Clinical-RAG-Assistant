9.15 Personnel Training and Evaluation  
The technical supervisor ensures that persons performing laboratory tests receive appropriate 
training. The performance of new personnel performing laboratory tests is evaluated 6 months after 
initial training conducted by staff deemed proficient in the laboratory task, and 6 months afterward for the first year. Assessments are performed annually thereafter. The evaluation shal l consist of, at 
the minimum, the six elements of:  
• Direct observation of test performance, including study participant preparation 
(if applicable), specimen handling, specimen processing, and testing;
