The clinical labora tory scientist  is responsible for inspecting the centrifuges at the beginning of each

 Centrifuge Function  
The Beckman Coulter Allegra X -15R centrifuge is a  refrigerated benchtop centrifuge that generates 
centrifugal forces required for a wide variety of applications. Together with the Beckman Coulter 
rotors designed for use in this centrifuge, the centrifuge applications include the following:
