 Inserting the Battery  
Charge the battery in the pipette. Make sure the battery is plugg ed in on the Xplorer pipettes. The 
battery should be charged by using the charging stand with the original power unit. A new battery 
does not attain its full capacity until several complete charging/discharging cycles have been 
completed.
