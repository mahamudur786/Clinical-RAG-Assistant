If patients with known or suspected airborne infections (e.g. pulmonary TB) are 
cohorted in a common area, and if multiple patients are to be visited sequentially, it may be advisable to wear a single particulate respirator for the duration of the activity. This type of use requires that the respirator should not be removed at any time during the activity, and that the user does not touch the respirator.
