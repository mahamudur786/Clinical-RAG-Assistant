x Prerequisites for isolation
R A handwashing sink and running water should be available at the entrance of each room 
to facilitate handwashing.
R Cover the mattress and pillows with an impervious cover such as Mackintosh so that it 
can easily be damp dusted. Clean gowns should always be available.
R Separate urinals, bedpans and thermometers/ BP apparatus are to be used for each 
patient.
R Bins lined with the appropriate colour-coded plastic liner should be available in each room 
for disposal of biomedical waste.
R Rooms should be well lit, and isolated according to disease conditions.
x Cleaning procedure for isolation room
R Linen should be stripped from the bed with care taken not to shake the linen during this
