Function of the various items of surgical attire
Gloves: Protect the patient from organisms on the surgeonâ€™s hand and also protects the 
surgeon from contact with the blood and tissues of the patient.
Gown and waterproof apron: Protects the patient from organisms on the body surface and 
clothes of the surgical team and protects the clothes and body surface of the surgeon from the blood and tissues of the patient.
Mask: Protects the patient against microorganisms expelled during breathing, talking, 
laughing and coughing. It also protects the surgeon (the mouth and nose) from splashes of blood and secretions.
Eye protection or visors, these protect the eyes of the surgeon from splashes of blood and 
secretions.
