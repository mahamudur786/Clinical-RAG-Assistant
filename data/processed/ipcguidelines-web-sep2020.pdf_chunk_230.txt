Indirect transmission
This involves the transfer of an infectious agent through a contaminated intermediate object (fomite) or person. These include: 
x hands of HCWs;
x clothing after care of a patient colonized or infected with an infectious agent, 
which can then be transmitted to subsequent patients;
x patient-care devices that are shared between patients without cleaning and 
disinfection; and
x environmental surfaces that are inadequately disinfected.
