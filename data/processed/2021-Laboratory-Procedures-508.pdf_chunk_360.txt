5.8 Quality Control  (QC) 
Run negative and low positive urine controls containing hCG at concentrations of 0 and 
25 mIU/m L, respectively, just prior to or concurrently with the first examinee pregnancy test each 
exam day, and when putting a new lot into use. The pregnancy QC module contains the following 
information: 
• The session ID number links to the stand number, stand location, and date, including 
day, month, and year. The technologist ID is automatically captured. 
• Kit lot number and expiration date fill in automatically from the prior QC report. 
Update or manually enter this information if necessary.
