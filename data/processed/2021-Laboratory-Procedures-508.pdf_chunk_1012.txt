10.4.7 Mettler Toledo Scale  
The Mettler Toledo PL 602E scale is a very sensitive -precision weighing instrument with a weighing 
limit of 610 grams. Handle the scale with care at all times. Do not weigh anything on the scale other 
than urine cups and specime ns. The weigh pan will get dirty during use. Clean the weigh pan as 
needed by removing the metal plate from the scale and cleaning using the NHANES COVID- 19
