1. Standards for incineration
All incinerators shall meet the following operating and emission standards.
A. Operating standards
1. Combustion efﬁ  ciency (CE) shall be at least 99.00%. 
2. The combustion efﬁ  ciency is computed as follows:

3. The temperature of the primary chamber shall be a minimum of 800 °C and the secondary 
chamber shall be minimum of 1050 °C ± 500 °C.
4. The secondary chamber gas residence time shall be at least two seconds.
