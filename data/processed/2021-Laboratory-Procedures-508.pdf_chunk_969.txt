.4.1.4 Care and Maintenance for Buckets  
• Clean the buckets at the end of each stand  with a 1:10 dilution of Beckman Solution 
555, and then rinse thoroughly and dry completely.

Lubricant as described under Rotor Care and Maintenance .

10.4. 1.5 Care and Maintenance for the Aerosolve® Canisters  
• Remove the O -ring from the canister before cleaning.  
• Clean the canister  and O- ring at the end of each stand with a 1:10 dilution of Beckman 
Solution 555, then rinse thoroughly and dry completely.
