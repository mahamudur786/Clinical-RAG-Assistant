6.8.3  Cancel Automatic Shutdown  
At certain times , automatic shutdown will have to be cancelled.  To cancel automatic shutdown: 
• From the Daily Checks screen , select Auto Configuration.  
• Select Configure Shutdown.  
• Remove the checkmark  from the Enable Automatic Shutdown box. 
To perform a manual shutdown when Auto Configuration is disabled, see the information that 
follows (Section 6.8.4) .
