9-4  Laboratory Procedures 2021  
  
Laboratory reports are released only to the authorized SP. Each method includes a description of 
actions taken, such as when results are markedly elevated or are “ panic values. ” The SP is contacted 
immediately by the MEC clinician with such results. 
9.5.3 List of Methods and Method Details  
NHANES maintains a LPM, which includes detailed descriptions of each analytical procedure, 
including analytical sensitivity, specificity, and accuracy, interfering substances, and other 
information. 
9.5.4 Format of the Test Report  
The test report includes: 
• Name and address of NCHS; 
• Test result with a footnote attached representing the name and address where the 
laboratory test was performed;  
• Units of measurement;
