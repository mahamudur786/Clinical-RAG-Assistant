Procedures and practices for IPC 47
4. Prevention of injuries from sharps
Handling sharps
Handling sharps (needles, scalpels, etc.) is one of the most hazardous activities 
carried out by HCWs in the course of their duties. Sharps should be handled with extreme caution to avoid injuries during use, disposal or reprocessing.
x Used needles must not be recapped by hand; if necessary, use the single hand 
“scoop” method.
x Used needles should not be bent or broken after use.
x Used sharps should be disposed of immediately in designated puncture-proof
