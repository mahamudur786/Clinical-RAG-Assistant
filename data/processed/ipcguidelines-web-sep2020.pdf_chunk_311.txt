Hygienic transportation of food 
x The conveyance must be clean, maintained and repaired so that there is no 
food contamination. 
x Adequate temperature must be maintained for all types of foods and must be 
protected from pests, foreign matter and environmental pollution. 
x The conveyance must be used only to carry food and for no other purpose.
Outbreaks
Bacterial food poisoning (acute gastroenteritis) is an infection or intoxication
