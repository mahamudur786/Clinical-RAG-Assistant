xii National Guidelines for Infection Prevention and Control in Healthcare Facilities
Healthcare-associated infections (HAI), also referred to as 
“nosocomial” or “hospital” infections, occur in a patient during the process of care in a hospital or other healthcare facility and were not present or incubating at the time of admission. HAI can affect patients in any type of setting where they receive care and can also appear after discharge; and include occupational infections among staff.
