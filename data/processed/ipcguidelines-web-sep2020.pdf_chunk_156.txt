Box 3.1. Prerequisites for an effective IPC programme
x Policies and guidelines, including best practices and standard operating 
procedures (SOPs)
x Adequately trained and motivated human resources 
x Continuous and adequate supply of personal protective equipment (PPE)
x Antimicrobial use policy and links with the antimicrobial stewardship programme 
(AMSP) 
x Integration with activities of the quality and safety department
