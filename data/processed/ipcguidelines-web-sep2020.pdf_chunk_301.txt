Foods prepared to be served cold are cooled from their preparation temperature to 
4 °C or below. The cooling period should not exceed 4 hours. Hot foods are held at an internal temperature of 63 °C or above. Both hot and cold food items should be transported in food trolleys in such a manner that appropriate temperatures are maintained during the transportation of the food.
