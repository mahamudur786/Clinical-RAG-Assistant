Selection and safe use of PPE
The type of PPE should be selected on the basis of estimated risk of contamination of the HCW’s hands, clothing or other areas of the body by blood, body ﬂ  uid, excretions or secretions of the patient. The route of transmission of the infectious agent is an important factor in selecting the PPE.
