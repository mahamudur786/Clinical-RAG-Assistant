80 National Guidelines for Infection Prevention and Control in Healthcare Facilities
Personnel should wash exposed portions of their arms and hands with soap and 
water before starting work. 
Personal habits
Staff should keep short nails and not wear religious bands, wrist bands, rings, bracelets and nail polish on duty. 
x Keep clothing free from obvious dirt and food spills.
x Use hair nets (hair restraints) while on duty.
x Use serving tools/ utensils to handle food whenever possible.
x Do not consume food or drinks in the food preparation or serving areas.
x Do not use tobacco products in any form while engaged in the preparation or 
serving of food.
