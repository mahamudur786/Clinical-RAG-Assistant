Risk factors
x Age: very young or very old
x Coronary bypass surgery
x Abdominal surgery
x Existing pulmonary, neurological disease
x Decreased clearance of respiratory secretions due to coma, sedation, etc.
x Invasive devices bypassing natural defences as in mechanical ventilation, 
intubation, tracheostomy, enteral feeding
x Medications such as antibiotics, antacids, immunosuppressive agents and 
chemotherapy
