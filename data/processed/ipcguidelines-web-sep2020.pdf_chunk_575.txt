Occupational health programme
An occupational health programme is essential for an effective IPC programme and has implications for patient safety. The components of such a programme are: 
x Evaluation for general health of employees, including infectious diseases at 
entry, periodically as required
x Screening for vaccination for childhood communicable diseases (measles, 
rubella, chickenpox, diphtheria, pertussis, tetanus) 
x Hepatitis B status and immunization
