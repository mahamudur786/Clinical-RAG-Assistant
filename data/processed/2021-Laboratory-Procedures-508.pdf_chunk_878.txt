 9-40  Laboratory Procedures 2021  
  
Documented competency assessment is required for individuals fulfilling the following personnel 
responsibilities outlined in Subpart M of the CLIA regulations: clinical consultant (CC), technical consultant (TC), technical supervisor (TS), and testing personnel (TP). Clinical consultants and 
technical consultants  who perform testing on patient specimens are required to have the six required 
procedures in their competency assessment in addition to a competency assessment based on their 
regulatory responsibilities.
