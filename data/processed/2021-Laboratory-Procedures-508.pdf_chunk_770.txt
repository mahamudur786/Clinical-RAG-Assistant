 9-2 Laboratory Procedures 2021  
  
A record of the test result report is maintained electronically by Westat (the primary NHANES 
contractor) and by NCHS for an indefinite period. NHANES MEC laboratory staff may also perform other testing at the request of NCHS. 
9.3.2 Laboratory Tests  
The MEC clinician reports “ critical ” or abnormally high/low CBC results to the SP. The report 
includes laboratory tests that will be performed. The MEC clinician involved in the measurement may also confer with the SP to obtain any other pertinent information that might fac ilitate the 
laboratory measurement process.
