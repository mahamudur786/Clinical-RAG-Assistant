3.4 Gaining Cooperation  
The coordinator will introduce the SP to the examination and briefly explain the examination 
process. The coordinator can answer any general questions the SP has about venipuncture; however, the phlebotomist must be prepared to answer all of the questions the SP poses about the venipuncture procedure. In addition, the phlebotomist must convey the importance of cooperation 
in the venipuncture component of the examination.
