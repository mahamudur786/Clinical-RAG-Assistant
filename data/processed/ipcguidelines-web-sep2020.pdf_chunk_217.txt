x Single rooms 
 Single rooms reduce the risk of transmission of infection from the source 
patient to others by reducing direct or indirect contact transmission. Single rooms should have: R hand-washing facilities
R toilet and bathroom facilities. 
x Anterooms 
 Single rooms used for isolation purposes may include an anteroom to support 
the use of PPE.
Placement with regular admissions
x A room should be cleaned before admitting a patient. There should be a policy 
for cleaning the room (i) after patient discharge (terminal cleaning) and (ii) before admission.
