Risk assessment
Risk assessment of all activities being performed in the laboratory is mandatory to be able to manage and reduce the risks to those working in the laboratory. Risk must be assessed taking into account the adequacy of any existing controls and whether or not the risk is acceptable. Risk assessment involves systematically reviewing the work to see:
x which biological agents may be present; 
x identifying the hazardous characteristics of known infectious or potentially 
infectious agent or material (see the classiﬁ  cation of infective microorganisms 
by risk group); 
x activities in the laboratory that can result in a person’s exposure to an agent
