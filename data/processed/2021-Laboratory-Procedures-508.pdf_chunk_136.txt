2.12 Warning and Error Messages  
Throughout the course of an examination, warning and error messages may appear when an attempt 
is made to perform an action the program cannot execute or that requires confirmation to continue the action. The message normally appears with statements explainin g the error condition. Complying 
with the error message statement will normally remedy the error. When required to confirm an 
action, such as deleting records, be sure the action is necessary because, in most cases, it is 
irreversible.
