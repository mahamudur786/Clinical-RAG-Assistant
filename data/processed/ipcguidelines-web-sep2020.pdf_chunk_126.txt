Responsibilities of the nurse in-charge of ward/unit
x Maintain hygiene, consistent with hospital policies and good nursing practices. 
x Monitor aseptic techniques, including hand hygiene, standard precautions and 
other precautions. 
x Report promptly any evidence of infection in patients to the attending physician.
x Initiate patient isolation and order culture specimens from any patient showing 
signs of a communicable disease, when the physician is not immediately available.
x Limit patient exposure to infections from visitors, hospital staff, other patients or 
devices/ equipment used for diagnosis or treatment.
x Maintain adequate supply of ward equipment, drugs and patient-care supplies.
