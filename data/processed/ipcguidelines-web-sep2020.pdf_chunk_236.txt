Placement of patient with transmissible disease
Appropriate patient placement is a signiﬁ  cant component of isolation precaution. A 
patient with a highly transmissible disease (e.g. chicken pox, TB, measles) should be placed in a single room with handwashing and toilet facility and airborne isolation.
