 10-52  Laboratory Procedures 2021   
Observe the following points: 
 Handling  
• Use the Xplorer Plus only when a pipe tte tip is attached.  
• Do not lay down the Xplorer Plus when a filled pipette tip is attached. 
• After liquid has been aspirated, press the Actuate key only when you are sure the liquid 
will be correctly dispensed. 
• When the pipette is switched on and when the Reset key is pressed, the piston may 
move. Please ensure beforehand that the liquid in the pipette tip will be dispensed correctly.  
• Emergency stop: pressing the Reset key can stop a moving piston. 
• Operate the Xplorer Plus at temperatures between 17 ºC-25ºC. Operate at a maximum
