8.11.1  Pack the Boxed Specimens  
Store the specimen  vessels in 2 -, 3-, and 5- inch storage boxes. Separate the vessels with cardboard 
grids or foam inserts. Secure the top of the cardboard box to the bottom with a rubber band. Place one absorbent sheet under the rubber band. Select the appropriate size STP Biohazard Packaging 
system: STP 710- 9” x 9 ” x 2 ” and 10” x 10” x 2 ” STP730 for 9” x 9 ” x 3 ”, 5” x 5 ” x 3,” 7” x 7 ” x 
3” and STP- 740 for 5” x 5 ” x 7 ” with foam insert. Place the contain er in the plastic biohazard bag.
