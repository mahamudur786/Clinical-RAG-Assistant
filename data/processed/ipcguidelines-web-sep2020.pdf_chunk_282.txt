Safe water and food
Provision of safe water
HCFs should have adequate and continuous supply of safe water. Safe water 
should be available for drinking, handwashing, food preparation, personal hygiene, medical activities, cleaning and laundry. The requirement of water for hospitals with a bed strength of less than 100 is 340 litres/bed/day, whereas for hospitals with bed strength over 100 beds, it is 400 litres/bed/day.
