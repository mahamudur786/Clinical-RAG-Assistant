9.6.8 Proficiency Testing  
Participation in the College of American Pathologists (CAP) proficiency testing (PT) program is part 
of the comprehensive quality control program. Each MEC submits results for CAP specimens when scheduled for evaluation. CAP compares the results to established values and issues a report. A schedule of which MEC is receiving each survey is disseminated at the beginning of each year.  
The laboratory will satisfactorily participate in the HCFA -approved PT program for each method
