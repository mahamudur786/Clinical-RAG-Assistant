5.3 Computerization: Integrated Survey  Information System 
(ISIS)  
The MEC -automated ISIS system captures all data and stores it electronically at Westat ’s home 
office in Rockville, Maryland. Access the Pregnancy QC module to enter the quality control results. 
The session number, clinical laboratory scientist ID, and run number are automatically captured; enter the kit lot number and expiration date as well as the urine control lot and expiration dates. 
Record control results and save them to the database. All pregnancy quality control results are 
immediately available electronically at the home office. All data is  backed up and stored at Westat’ s 
home office.
