Postoperative factors 
x A surgical incision that has been closed by primary closure should be covered 
with a sterile dressing till 48 hours. For an incision with delayed primary closure or healing by second intention, the incision should be packed with a sterile dressing. Use of an advanced dressing over a standard dressing on primarily closed surgical wounds is not recommended.
x The wound drain should be removed when clinically indicated.
