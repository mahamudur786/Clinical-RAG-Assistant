A. Surveillance of healthcare-associated infections
Surveillance of HAIs allows the health system to (i) estimate the burden of diseases 
in terms of cases reported, deaths occurred and costs incurred; (ii) detect outbreaks and emerging pathogens and pattern of resistance; and (iii) monitor the quality of IPC measures/ strategies.
Surveillance of HAIs is a basic requirement for organizing and maintaining an 
effective IPC programme and to substantially reduce morbidity and mortality.
