MONEY AND VALUABLES 
When you are admitted, valuables such as 
money or jewelry should be taken to t he 
cashier's office located on the first floor where 
they will be placed in a locked vault. You will 
receive a receipt. Cashier's hours are 9 a.m. to 
5 p.m. Monday thru Friday. If you expect to be 
discharged on a weekend, evening, or holiday, 
withdraw your valuables in advance. You may 
call the cashier' s office at 301-496-2654. If you 
are going to be discharged before 9 a.m., be 
sure to remove your property before 5 p.m.
