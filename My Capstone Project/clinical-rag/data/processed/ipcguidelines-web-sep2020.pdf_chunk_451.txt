x Laboratory coats should be decontaminated and laundered regularly (never 
taken home for laundering). 
x Comfortable, water repellent closed shoes with non-skid soles should be worn 
and must enclose the entire foot. 
x Long, dangling jewellery is not permitted in the laboratory.
x Long hair and beards must be tied back to avoid contamination and 
interference with laboratory work. 
x A spare, clean laboratory coat must be available in case of a spill or an 
emergency.
Good personal habits
x Wear proper attire and protective clothing as described above. 
x Wash hands after entering and before leaving the laboratory.
