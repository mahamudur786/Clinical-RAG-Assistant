Liver Ultrasound Transient Elastography , and DEXA (Body Composition) due to pregnancy status. 
At the coordinator application, the component status for Liver Ultrasound Transient Elastography and DEXA defaults to “ Not Do ne” with the comment “Blocked: Not Done [SP pregnant].”
