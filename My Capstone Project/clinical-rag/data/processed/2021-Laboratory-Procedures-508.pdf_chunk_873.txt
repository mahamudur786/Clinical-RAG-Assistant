9.14.10  Quality Assurance Review with Staff  
The technical supervisor and laboratory director prepare a report of the results of quality audits. The technical supervisor and laboratory director review pertinent information with appropriate staff to ensure that effective remedial actions are taken. The technical supervisor and laboratory director also 
notify staff members who have been involved in the audit if their performance was acceptable and 
no remedial action is needed. At least once every 6 mo nths, the technical supervisor and laboratory 
director review  the results of quality audits.
