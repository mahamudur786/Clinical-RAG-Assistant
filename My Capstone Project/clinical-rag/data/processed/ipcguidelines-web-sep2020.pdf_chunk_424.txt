Outbreaks of HCV infections in HD facilities, which occur frequently, have often been 
due to poor infection control practices. Sources of infection could be contaminated water, equipment and environmental surfaces in the treatment area and patients with infections who pose a risk to other nearby patients.
IPC programme in the dialysis unit
Patients undergoing haemodialysis are at increased risk of HAIs. Therefore, the IPC programme is essential for HD units. It includes multiple interventions which are designed to reduce the risk of infection.
A doctor or a senior nurse working in the unit should be given responsibilities of IPC 
activities. The role of this link person includes
x Monitoring of IPC practices
x training of new staff and ongoing training of all staff
