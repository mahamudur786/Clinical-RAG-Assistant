Healthcare-associated infections and their surveillance 151
x Use of protocols for intensive perioperative blood glucose control for both 
diabetic and non-diabetic adult patients undergoing surgical procedures to reduce the risk of SSI is suggested. 
x Maintenance of adequate circulating volume control/ normovolaemia. Fluid 
therapy should be goal directed.
x Triclosan-coated sutures are suggested independent of the type of surgery.
x Use of warming devices in the OT and during the surgical procedure should be 
used for patient body warming for reducing SSI.
x Wound protector devices are suggested in clean-contaminated, contaminated 
and dirty abdominal surgical procedures for reducing SSI.
x Irrigation of the incisional wound with an aqueous PVP-I solution before closure
