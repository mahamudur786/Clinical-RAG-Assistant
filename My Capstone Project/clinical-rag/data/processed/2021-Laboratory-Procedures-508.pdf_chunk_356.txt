Use controls in the same manner as SP specimens in accordance with the protocol provided in the Sure-Vue™ hCG Control Sets directional insert. 
– Low Positive Control: Human urine containing approximately 25 mIU of hCG/mL and 0.2 percent sodium azide as a preservative (1 x 5 m L). 
– High Positive Control: Human urine containing approximately 250 mIU of hCG/mL and 0.2 percent sodium azide as a preservative (1 x 5 m L). 
– Negative Control: Contains 0.2 percent sodium azide as a preservative (1 x 5 
mL). 
• Storage and stability 
– Store the urine control set at 2-8 °C (35 -46°F) until the expiration date printed on 
the label.
