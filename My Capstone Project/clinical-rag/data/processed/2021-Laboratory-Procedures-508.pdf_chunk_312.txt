4.5 Manually Documenting Urine Collection 
4.5.1 Manually Document Urine Collection w ith Barcode Scanning

in the Urine Collection module within the laboratory application. Place the urine collection cup on 
the scale and cover with the clear acrylic scale cover. Scan the barcode on the urine cup.
