Procedure for septum vials
x Wipe the access diaphragm (septum) with 70% alcohol on a swab before 
piercing the vial, and allow to air dry before inserting a device into the bottle.
x Use a sterile syringe and needle for each insertion into a multi-dose vial.
x Never leave a needle in a multi-dose vial.
Practical guidance on administering injections
Aseptic techniques should be followed for all injections.
