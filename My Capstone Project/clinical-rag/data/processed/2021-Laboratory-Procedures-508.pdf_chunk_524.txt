6.19 Interpretation of Results and Remedial Action 
6.19.1  Sample Person Hemoglobin and Hematocrit Review and Remedial 
Actions  
Review all results to make sure the hemoglobin and hematocrit are acceptable. The hematocrit should be approximately three times the hemoglobin. If an “H&H Does Not Match Flag” is displayed on both runs of an SP, repeat the run. Report out of range results if all runs are similarly flagged. Delete all but two of the runs before averaging.
