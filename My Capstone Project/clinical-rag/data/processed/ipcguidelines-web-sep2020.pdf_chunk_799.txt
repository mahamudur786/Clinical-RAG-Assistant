Eye protection or visors, these protect the eyes of the surgeon from splashes of blood and 
secretions.
Cap: Protects the patient from organisms shed from the hair and skin of the surgeon, should 
cover all the hair
Footwear; these should be made of sturdy, washable material with closed toes to protect 
the feet from splashes, and injury due to falling instrument If footwear are not available shoe covers can be worn. These are disposable or reusable.
