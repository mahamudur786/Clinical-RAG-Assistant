9.9 Equipment Maintenance and Function Checks  
9.9.1 Introduction  
Laboratory equipment is regularly checked to ensure acceptable performance. Maintenance 
(including preventive maintenance ), and external checks are documented in the Laboratory and 
Phlebotomy Quality Control modules in ISIS. The frequency of maintenance and daily checks 
follows the manufacturer’ s recommendations, when available. The manufacturer ’s recommendations 
are included in the manuals that are located in notebooks in the laboratory.
