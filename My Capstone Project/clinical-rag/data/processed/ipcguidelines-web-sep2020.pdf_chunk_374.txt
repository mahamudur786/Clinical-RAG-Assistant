x Use sterile sleeve to protect pulmonary catheters during insertion.
Skin preparation x Prepare clean skin with an antiseptic (70% alcohol, tincture of iodine, an 
iodophor or chlorhexidine gluconate) before insertion of a peripheral venous catheter.
x Prepare clean skin with a >0.5% chlorhexidine preparation with alcohol before 
insertion of a central venous catheter or peripheral arterial catheter and during dressing changes. If there is a contraindication to chlorhexidine, use tincture of iodine, an iodophor or 70% alcohol.
