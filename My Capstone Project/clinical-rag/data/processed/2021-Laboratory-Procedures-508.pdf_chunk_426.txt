6.8.1  Daily Shutdown  
Beckman Coulter recommends that the SPM remain in cleaner for at least 30 minutes every 
24 hours. If the SPM has power and is in Shutdown but the pneumatics are  off, automatic cycling 
occurs every 24 hours to prevent flow cell and sample line clogging.
