Recommendations for the prevention of SSIs
The following recommendations are important for preparing SSIs and are based on 
WHO global guidelines on the prevention of SSIs.114,115 
Preoperative recommendations
x Whenever possible, efforts shall be made to identify and treat all infections 
remote to the surgical site before elective operation and postpone elective operations on patients with remote site infections until the infection has resolved. 
x Ensure adequate control of serum blood glucose levels in all diabetic patients.
