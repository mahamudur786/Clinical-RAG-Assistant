210 National Guidelines for Infection Prevention and Control in Healthcare Facilities
Annex 6: Policy for visitors and attendants
The following points should be considered for visitors and attendants.
1. Limit visitors to persons necessary for patient’s emotional well-being and care. Visitor 
restrictions apply to all visitors, including staff members and their families. Visitors should limit their movement within the facility. Visitors should not sit on hospital beds or put their feet on beds.
