Programming – PIP 
Step 1  Select the pipetting mode PIP. Turn the dial until the PIP choice appears.  
Step 2  Press the small blue “ EDIT ” button on the left.  
Step 3 Select the desired volume . 
If you need to adjust the volume, use the blue toggle to reach desired volume.  
Step 4  Set aspiration speed to 8. Hit small blue key to “ Next. ” 
Step 5  Set dispensing speed to 8. Hit small blue key to “ End.”
