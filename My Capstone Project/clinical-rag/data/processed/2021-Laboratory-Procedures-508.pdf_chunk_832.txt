9.8.3 Approval and Record Maintenance  
Each procedure is reviewed, approved, signed, and dated by the clinical consultant and technical 
supervisor and laboratory director of NHANES. Procedures are reapproved, signed, and dated if the director changes. Each change in a procedure is approved, signed, and dated by the current director.

documentation is maintained for 7 years after discontinuance of the method.
