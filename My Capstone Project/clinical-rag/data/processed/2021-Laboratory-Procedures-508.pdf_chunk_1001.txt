1.3.1 Start up 
Turn on cabinet blower and lights, check air intake and exhaust portals of the cabinet to make sure 
they are unobstructed. 
Good procedure includes the decontamination or wipe down of cabinet surfaces with chemical 
disinfectant before work commences. This practice eliminates the nee d for UV lights, whose 
primary utility in this application is inactivation of surface contamination because  the filters 
effectively remove all airborne contaminants. UV lights, therefore, are not recommended in the LFBSC.
