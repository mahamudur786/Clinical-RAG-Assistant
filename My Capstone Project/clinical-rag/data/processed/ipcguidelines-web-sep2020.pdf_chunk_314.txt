Biomedical waste
The modern hospital is a complex multidisciplinary system, which consumes several types of items for the delivery of patient care. All these products consumed in the hospital have some unusable leftovers, which are called healthcare/ clinical waste as they are generated as a result of some clinical activity. Biomedical waste is a broader term applied to waste generated in the diagnosis, treatment or immunization of humans and animals, in research or in the production and testing of biological products.
