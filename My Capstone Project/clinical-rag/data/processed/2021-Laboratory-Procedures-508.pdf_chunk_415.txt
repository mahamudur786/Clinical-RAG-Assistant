6.7.1  Reagents and Supplies  
DxH r eagents , including storage and handling requirements, are listed below . 
• Coulter DxH diluent (store at 2–40°C)  
– Coulter DxH diluent is shipped in ready- to-use containers. 
– Opened containers are stable for 60 days.  Do not use this product beyond the 
expiration date. 
– Coulter DxH diluent is a cyanide-free, isotonic-buffered saline solution. 
– Coulter DxH diluent dilutes the specimen, is used for rinsing SPM components 
between sample analyses, and provides a sheath stream to  transport the specimen 
through the flow cell. 
• Coulter DxH Lyse reagent (store at 2 –40°C)
