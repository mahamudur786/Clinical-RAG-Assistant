Aprons and gowns 
International guidelines recommend that protective clothing (apron or gown) should 
be worn by all HCWs when:
x there is close contact with the patient, materials or equipment that may lead to 
contamination of skin, uniforms or other clothing with infectious agents; and
x there is a risk of contamination with blood, body substances, secretions or 
excretions (except sweat).
