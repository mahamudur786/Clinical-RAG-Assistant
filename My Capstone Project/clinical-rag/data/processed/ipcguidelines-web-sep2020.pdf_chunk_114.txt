Infection control nurse
A full-time nursing staff should be appointed as the ICN. She/ he should have training 
in IPC, preferably through an accredited course. The duties of ICNs are primarily associated with ensuring the practice of IPC by HCWs. She/ he is a member of the IPC team and is responsible for liaising between the microbiology laboratory and the wards, ICU, OTs, etc. to identify problems and implement solutions.
