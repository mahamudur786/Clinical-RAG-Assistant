3. Routine vaginal cleansing with chlorhexidine during labour for the

4. Routine vaginal cleansing with chlorhexidine during labour in women

for prevention of early neonatal GBS infection. 
5. Intrapartum antibiotic administration to women with GBS colonization 
is recommended for prevention of early neonatal GBS infection. 
6. Routine antibiotic prophylaxis during the second or third trimester 
for all women with the aim of reducing infectious morbidity is not recommended. 
7. Routine antibiotic administration is not recommended for women in 
preterm labour with intact amniotic membranes.
