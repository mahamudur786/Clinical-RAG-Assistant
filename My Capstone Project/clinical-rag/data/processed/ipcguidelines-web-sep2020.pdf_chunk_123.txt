Pharmacist and pharmacology department
Hospital pharmacists are responsible for dispensing and procurement of medical products and supplies used in the hospital. The pharmacist should be an active member of the HICC and play a major role in the antimicrobial stewardship programme. 
Responsibilities
x Dispense antiseptics and disinfectants and maintain relevant records (potency, 
incompatibility, conditions of storage and deterioration).
x Obtain and store vaccines or sera, and make them available as appropriate.
x Maintain records of antibiotics distributed to all departments, and the analysis 
of antibiotic consumption.
