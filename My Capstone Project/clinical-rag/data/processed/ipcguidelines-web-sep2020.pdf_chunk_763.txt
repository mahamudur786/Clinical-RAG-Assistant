It shall be the duty of every operator to:
a. Take all necessary steps to ensure that the biomedical waste collected from the occupier 
is transported, handled, stored, treated and disposed of, without any adverse effect to the human health and the environment, in accordance with these rules and guidelines issued by the Central Government or, as the case may be, the Central Pollution Control Board from time to time; 
b. Ensure timely collection of biomedical waste from the occupier as prescribed under these 
rules; 
c. Establish bar coding and global positioning system for handling of biomedical waste in 
accordance with the guidelines issued by the Central Pollution Control Board by 27 March 2019;
