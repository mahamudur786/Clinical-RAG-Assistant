Data to be collected
x Clinical chart review for patients having fever and on antimicrobial therapy 
(which is a sensitive indicator of HAIs).
x Review the microbiology reports if available.
x Data collected for a probable case includes patient number, age, gender, 
location, associated comorbidity such as diabetes, type of infection, site and severity of infection, investigations done for infection.
x In case of an SSI, whether surgery was performed at the hospital within the 
preceding 30 days (or within 1 year if an implant was in place), the date of surgery and type of surgery are recorded.
Denominators 
x Number of patients present/ admitted in the ward on that day
x For device-related HAIs: total device days of the existing patients in the ward 
x Reporting and feedback
