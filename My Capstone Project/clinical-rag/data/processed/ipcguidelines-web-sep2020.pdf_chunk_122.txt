Responsibilities
x Provide quality patient-care services to minimize infection in accordance with 
the recommended IPC practices as per HCF policy and guidelines (e.g. hand hygiene, standard precautions, aseptic procedures, isolation).
x Serve on the HICC.
x Support the IPC team.
x Ensure collection of appropriate microbiological specimens when an infection is 
suspected.
x Notify infected/ HAI cases to the IPC team.
x Comply with the antibiotic policy and support the antibiotic stewardship 
programme.
x Advise patients, visitors and staff on measures to prevent the transmission of 
infection.
