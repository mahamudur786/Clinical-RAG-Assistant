10.5.1 Blood Equipment and Supplies  
The blood processing area includes two laboratory- grade refrigerators, four laboratory -grade 
freezers, two refrigerated centrifuges, one data terminal, a laminar flow hood, and one specimen 
rocker. This area also includes counter space for processing blood. Extra processing supplies are 
stored in cabinets and/or the trailer compartments. Exhibits 10 -11 and 10- 12 list nonconsumable 
and consumable supplies for blood processing.
