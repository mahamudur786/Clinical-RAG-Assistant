Gloves
After wearing the sterile gown, the sterile gloves are worn. Sterile surgical gloves should be worn in the following way.
x The outer package of gloves is opened by the assistant without touching the inner wrapper.
x Open inner glove wrapper exposing the cuffed gloves with palm up.
x Pick up one glove by the cuff touching only the inner portion of the cuff.
