Measures to reduce the risk of infection in HD patients
In the haemodialysis setting, contact transmission plays a major role in transmission of blood-borne pathogens. Transmission occurs via hands of HCWs, contaminated with infected blood directly or indirectly from contaminated surfaces and equipment.
