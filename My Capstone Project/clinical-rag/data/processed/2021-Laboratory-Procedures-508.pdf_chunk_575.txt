6.27 Troubleshooting  
If the background f ails: 
• If WBC, RBC, Plt fails —zap apertures, prime sweep flow several times, shutdown as 
last resort.  
• If Differential fails —flush the flow cell.  
• If Hgb fails —clean BSV, Prime CBC Module, perform Hgb blank verification, 
Shutdown. 
If the Latro n fails: 
• D or N(AI2) and/or D or N(Lals) out —unlock DV x 3, flush flow cell x 3.
