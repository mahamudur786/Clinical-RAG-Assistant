Portal of exit
Portals of exit are necessary for the organism to exit the body of one person and be transmitted to another person. The portal of exit can be the excretions/secretions of the respiratory tract, gastrointestinal tract, genital tract, blood or any other body ﬂ  uid.
Modes of transmission

host. Transmission can occur by:
x Portal of contact  – direct contact through hands or indirectly through an 
inanimate object;
x Droplets  – large-sized droplets released by sneezing, coughing or even talking;
