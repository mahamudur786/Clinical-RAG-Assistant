Infectious waste
Infectious waste includes all healthcare/ clinical waste that has the potential to transmit viral, bacterial, fungal or parasitic disease. It includes both human and animal waste, waste generated in laboratories and veterinary practice. Hazardous waste is any waste with a potential to pose a threat to human health and life. Infectious waste is a part of hazardous waste.
