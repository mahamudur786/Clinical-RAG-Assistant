8.7 Shipping Overview  
The integrity of laboratory specimens is critical to NHANES. Storage temperatures have been 
established for each analyte in consultation with the National Center for Health Statistics (NCHS) 
and contract laboratories. It is critical to maintain these established conditions throughout specimen 
storage and shipping and until their receipt by the contract laboratories. Maintain these conditions by using documented procedures, which include using sufficient dry ice, refrigerant packs, and sending specimens by FedEx priority overnight. 
FedEx priority overnight guarantees next -business- day delivery by 10:30 am to most U.S. addresses , 
and by noon, 4:30 pm,  or 5 pm in remote areas. Delivery by FedEx can be delayed due to a weekend
