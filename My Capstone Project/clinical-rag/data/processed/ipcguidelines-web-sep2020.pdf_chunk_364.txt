Catheter-related bloodstream infection and CLABSI
Intravascular catheters are an indispensable part of modern healthcare, but 
their use puts patients at risk of local and systemic infections: local site infection, bloodstream infection (BSI), septic thrombophlebitis, endocarditis, and other metastatic infections.
