The overall objective is to perform phlebotomy on all eligible SPs. The coordinator must base the 
decision to assign an SP to phlebotomy on the greater goal of completing as many components as possible within the time constraints of the session, with phlebotomy as the highest priority component. Sometimes an SP insists on leaving the MEC before they have  been assigned to 
phlebotomy. It is always preferable to perform the venipuncture on this SP before they  leave the 
MEC. The coordinator and MEC manager can provide the best overall assessment of the status of a particular SP.
