2.2 Laboratory Heads -Up Display  
The laboratory heads-up display presents the sample persons (SPs) scheduled into the session, the modules for which they are eligible, and their current pr ocess status. Use the heads -up display to 
access modules.

Sample ID, Status (SC=Scheduled, LI=Logged -in, CI=Checked -in, BE=Being examined,
