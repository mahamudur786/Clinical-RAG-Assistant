3. Respiratory hygiene and cough etiquette52 
Respiratory hygiene and cough etiquette means the measures taken by a person having signs and symptoms of respiratory infection to contain respiratory secretions and prevent the transmission of the infection to other persons. The following measures are recommended:

x Perform hand hygiene after contact with respiratory secretions and
