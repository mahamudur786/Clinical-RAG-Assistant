Responsibilities of the CSSD manager
x Develop a procedure manual to document SOPs for all processes carried out 
in the CSSD. The manual should be approved by the HICC and reviewed at regular intervals.
x Oversee the various processes carried out in the department, namely cleaning, 
decontamination, disinfection, wrapping, sterilization, storage and distribution.
x Monitor the processes by using different methods such as physical, chemical 
and bacteriological according to the policy of the HCF.
x Ensure technical maintenance of the equipment according to national 
standards and manufacturers’ recommendations.
x Report any defect to the administration, maintenance, infection control and 
other appropriate personnel.
x Ensure appropriate separation of “clean” and “dirty” areas.
