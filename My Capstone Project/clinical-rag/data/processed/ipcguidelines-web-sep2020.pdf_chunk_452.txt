x Never eat, smoke, drink, chew gum, apply cosmetics, or adjust contact lenses 
while in the laboratory. 
x Mouth pipetting is prohibited, instead use pipetting bulbs. 
x Keep hands away from the mouth, nose, and eyes to prevent self-inoculation 
with infectious agents. 
x Do not put objects in the mouth (such as pens, pencils or pipettes). 
x Wear gloves when working with biological specimens. Change gloves when 
contaminated. 
x In preparing specimens, prevent aerosols and the resultant possible spread of 
infectious agents by R capping all tubes to be centrifuged prior to centrifugation;
R never open the lids of centrifuges until the centrifuge has come to a 
complete stop; and
