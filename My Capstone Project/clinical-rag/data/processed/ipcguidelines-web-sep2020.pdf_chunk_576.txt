Pre-employment assessment
Before being allowed to work in high-risk areas, all staff should be assessed and 
offered testing and/or vaccination for speciﬁ  c infectious diseases. Details of medical 
history, particularly for infectious diseases such as rubella, measles, mumps, chickenpox (varicella), hepatitis B, immune disorders and skin conditions, and for prior exposure to tuberculosis should be recorded.
