This document outlines the broad principles and practices of infection control that are 
essential for the prevention of infection. It provides the framework to strengthen the infrastructure and human resources needed to address IPC. The Hospital Infection Control Team and Hospital Infection Control Committees are critical mechanisms to systematically address IPC in any healthcare facility. The infection control nurses are the key front-line professionals for implementing IPC in any healthcare facility. Capacity development and training to develop the skills of healthcare staff will also be crucial in implementing these guidelines.
