Microbiological testing of water69
Water used for drinking, handwashing, cleaning and disinfection should be tested as decided by the HICC. The suggested frequency is every three months and additionally if the source of water is changed, or after major repairs of the water supply system, or when a waterborne outbreak is suspected.
