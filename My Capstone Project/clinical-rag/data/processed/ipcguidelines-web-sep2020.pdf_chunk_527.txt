Other intraoperative interventions
x Drapes and gowns: Both sterile disposable non-woven or sterile, reusable 
woven drapes and gowns can be used during surgical operations for the purpose of preventing SSI. Plastic adhesive incise drapes with or without antimicrobial properties for the purpose of preventing SSI are not recommended.
