1.3.3 Materials and Equipment  
The apparatus and materials should next be placed into the cabinet. Care must be exercised that no 
items be placed over the front intake grills. Materials should be arranged so that clean, dirty (used), 
and virus materials are well separated. Passage of contaminated materials over uninoculated cultures 
or clean glassware should be avoided and transfer of viable materials should be performed as deeply into the cabinet (away from open face) as possible.
