Steps involved in HAI outbreak investigation 
The following steps are very useful when investigating an outbreak. It is important 
to remember that they are provided as a guideline. Although the steps are listed sequentially the process of outbreak investigation may occur in a different order, often simultaneously or repeated many times as new information is received.
