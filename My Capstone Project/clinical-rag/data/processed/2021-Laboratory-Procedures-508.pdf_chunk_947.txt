10.3.6 Shipping Inventory Back to the War ehouse  
When shipping inventory back to the warehouse, print the Transfer Inventory to Warehouse Sheet,  
which is found on the portal . This  form looks similar to the End of Stand Count Sheets. Print your 
name in the Counted By field and mark only the items (with quantity being returned) in the count 
boxes that are being shipped back to the warehouse. Ship  the items to be returned along with the 
Transfer Inventory to Warehouse Inventory Sheet back to the warehouse. This information is entered into the Inventor y Management System by the warehouse manager and is used to adjust the 
stand inventory and usage information and to increase the warehouse inventory counts.
