1.9 Cross Training  
Cross training the laboratory staff and the phlebotomist increases an individual’ s skills and provides 
scheduling flexibility and a safety net during staff absences. The primary phlebotomist helps the 
laboratory  team with shipping and set  up and tear down. CLSs and MLTs  trained in phlebotomy 
(backup phlebotomists) work in phlebotomy on a routine basis to ensure competency and 
proficiency. The basi c underlying assumption is that the blood draw must never  be compromised.
