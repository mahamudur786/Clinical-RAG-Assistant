National rules for biomedical waste management
The Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change published the Biomedical 
Waste Management Rules on 28 March 2016. These rules superseded the Biomedical Waste (Management and Handling) Rules, 1998. The 2016 Rules have been amended in 2018 and 2019.
