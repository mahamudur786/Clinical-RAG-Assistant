The t echnical consultant for moderate complexity  testing  (42 CFR ยง493.1413(b)(8)) is responsible 
for performing and documenting competency assessments. The competency assessments may also 
be performed by other personnel who meet the regulatory qualification requirements for TC for moderate complexity testing.  
Evaluating and documenting competency of personnel responsible for testing is required at least 
semiannually during the first year the individual tests patient specimens. Thereafter, competency assessment must be performed at least annually. Competency assessm ent can be done throughout 
the entire year by coordinating it with routine practices and procedures to minimize impact on workload.
