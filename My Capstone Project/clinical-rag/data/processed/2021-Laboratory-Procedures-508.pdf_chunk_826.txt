9.7.2 Labeling of Reagents, Solutions, and Other Supplies  
Reagents, solutions, and other supplies are labeled to indicate the identity of contents, the 
concentration (if significant), the recommended storage requirements, the preparation and expiration date, laboratory s taff initials, and any other pertinent information required for proper use. Reagent,
