10.8 Power Loss and Laboratory Operations 
Three scenarios may occur if power is lost to the MEC. Scenario 1 occurs when power is lost during an exam session and there is no backup generator. Scenario 2 occurs when power is lost when the MEC is not conducting a session. Scenario 3 occurs when power is lost but there is a backup 
generator available. Procedures to address each scenario are described in detail.
