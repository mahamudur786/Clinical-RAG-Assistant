Provision of safe food and kitchen hygiene
The quality and quantity of food are key factors for patient convalescence. Ensuring safe food is an important service delivery in healthcare.
70 
The dietary department ensures that food prepared and served to patients, visitors 
and employees is received, stored, prepared and served in a manner that avoids contamination. The aim is to prevent food- and waterborne infections. The dietary department should have a manual in which all the procedures for preparation and handling of food are available.
The guidance document by the FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of 
India) on the food management system must be followed.
