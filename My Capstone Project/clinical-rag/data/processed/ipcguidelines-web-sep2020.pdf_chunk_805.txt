Daily cleaning procedure
x Before start of the ﬁ  rst case, at least one hour before:
R Damp dust with detergent–disinfectant all equipment, furniture and lights
R Wipe surgical light reﬂ  ector again with 70% alcohol to remove the ﬁ  lm left by the 
detergent
 Annexes 231
x Between cases:
R Place soiled towels, drapes and gowns in a clean laundry bag and send to laundry. Wet 
linen should be placed in plastic container so that bacteria do not pass through the moist material.
R Soiled instruments must be placed in disinfectant and then send to the cleaning area, this 
prevents occupational hazard to the cleaner.
