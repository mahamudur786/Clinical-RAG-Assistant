8. Generate a hypothesis and test the hypothesis
A hypothesis in the context of an outbreak investigation is an educated guess about 
an association between an exposure and the outcome of interest or disease which can be tested. Descriptive data collected in the previous step of the investigation provide information that is very useful in the development of hypotheses.
