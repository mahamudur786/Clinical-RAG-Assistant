DxH controls, including storage and handling requirements, are listed below:  
• Coulter 6C cell control (store at 2–8°C)  
– The Coulter 6C c ell control is an integrated control that enables monitoring of 
system performance and calibration status for all directly measured and calculated 
CBC, Diff, and NRBC parameters. Instructions for use are as follows: 
– Remove 6C c ell control vials from refrigerator and warm at ambient temperature 
for 10 to 15 minutes.  
– Roll the tube slowly between the palms of the hands eight times in an upright 
position. Invert the tube and slowly roll between the palms of the hands eight 
times. Then gently invert the tube eight times. Repeat.
