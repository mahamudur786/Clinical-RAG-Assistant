IPC in special units or situations 121
Defer or limit procedures that could facilitate spread of the infection. 
x Identify mechanisms of patient referral, sample transport – ambulance, sample 
packaging, reference laboratories.
x Ensure adequate protection of the hospital staff against infection and monitor 
staff health status continuously. Undertake staff vaccination and prophylaxis, training in IPC.
x Monitor IPC practices and modify policies as necessary. 
x Ensure communication of messages aimed at reinforcing IPC efforts among 
hospital staff, patients and visitors, and the community.
x Report and communicate with local public health authorities.
For IPC guidance on specific pandemic diseases refer to WHO documents
